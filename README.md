## Overview
Open a folder, skim RAW files at speed, mark keepers, and export only your selects.<br>
Zebra + Histogram and Faux HDR help check exposure and detail. Ratings and color labels are saved to XMP for use in other editors.


## Download
[simple-raw-picker-setup](https://github.com/recu3125/simpleRawPicker/releases/download/v1.0/simple-raw-picker-setup.zip)

## Quick Start

### 1) Prepare the source folder
- Put photos **directly under the folder**. Subfolders are ignored.
- RAW-only culling. Same-name JPEGs are auto-paired and copied on export.
- Ratings and color labels are saved to XMP.

### 2) Pick a folder on the start screen
- Use *Select Folder to Start*, or continue from *recent folders*.

### 3) Skim and pick
- Navigate with <kbd>←</kbd>/<kbd>→</kbd> or <kbd>A</kbd>/<kbd>D</kbd>. Toggle pick with <kbd>Space</kbd>.
- Zoom and pan with wheel and drag.
- <kbd>F</kbd> shows picked photos only.
- Check exposure/detail with : Zebra/Histogram <kbd>Q</kbd>, Faux HDR <kbd>E</kbd>.
- Ratings <kbd>1</kbd>–<kbd>5</kbd>, color labels <kbd>6</kbd>–<kbd>0</kbd> → saved to XMP.

### 4) Export
- Click **Complete** to export picked shots.
- RAW files copy to <code>_selected_raw</code> with matching XMP.
- Matching JPEGs copy to <code>_selected_jpeg</code>.
